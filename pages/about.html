ABOUT ME.

I am a graphic designer based in Los Angeles, California, driven by a passion for creative expression and visual storytelling. My journey in design began with a foundation in business, which I pursued for two years at the University of Oregon. My desire to explore the digital realm led me to transition to Los Angeles in late 2019. Here, at the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising (FIDM), I have honed my skills and immersed myself in the diverse disciplines of graphic design.

Graduating with a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, I bring to the table a versatile skill set encompassing branding, marketing, concept and product design, illustration, motion graphics, UX, and web design. Proficient in industry-standard tools such as the Adobe Creative Suite, as well as advanced software like Cinema 4D and Blender, I have developed a robust skill set that enables me to bring concepts to life in both two and three-dimensional spaces.
